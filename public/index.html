Who we are

Food Pharmacy Ghana is a privately held Health, Wellness & Fitness company (founded 2016) based in Tema Community 19. We teach practical habits that help you prevent lifestyle diseases.

Our belief

Food is medicine. We treat every meal as a conscious choice for health and long life.

Our focus

Maximizing nutrients while reducing food pollution so your family can thrive.

Our promise

Simple steps, real results, and a supportive community that makes healthy living sustainable.
