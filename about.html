Who we are

Food Pharmacy Ghana is a privately held Health, Wellness & Fitness company (founded 2016) based in Tema Community 19. We believe the food we eat is our medicine and we make a conscious effort to ensure it is nutritious and safe.

Our belief

Food is medicine. Every meal is an opportunity to support long-term health.

Our mission

Help families look better, feel better, and live healthier with sustainable daily habits.

Our community

Supportive education, free presentations, and guidance that makes healthy living practical.
